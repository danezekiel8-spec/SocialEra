Returns Policy

Returns are reviewed based on product condition, issue type, and supplier terms.

Because SocialEra sells a mix of directly managed and supplier-fulfilled products, return eligibility can vary by product category and fulfillment source. We strongly recommend reviewing product details before placing an order.

Returns Summary

Return request window Contact us within 7 days of delivery for damaged, incorrect, or materially defective items.
Change-of-mind returns Not all supplier-fulfilled items are eligible for change-of-mind returns.
Approval required Do not send an item back until you receive return instructions from SocialEra.

Eligible Return Reasons

  • The item arrived damaged.
  • The wrong product was delivered.
  • The item received is materially different from the product description.
  • A manufacturing defect is confirmed after review.

Items That May Not Be Returnable

  • Final-sale or clearance items if stated on the product page.
  • Products returned without prior approval.
  • Used, worn, washed, or altered items.
  • Supplier-fulfilled products that the supplier does not accept as change-of-mind returns.

How To Start A Return

Email support with your order number, a brief explanation of the issue, and clear photos where relevant. Once reviewed, we will confirm whether the item qualifies for return, replacement, store credit, or refund.

Refund Timing

If a refund is approved, it is typically issued back to the original payment method after the returned item is received or the issue is verified. Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.

Cancellations

Orders may only be canceled before processing begins. Because some items move quickly into supplier fulfillment, cancellation requests are not guaranteed once an order has entered processing.

Contact

Need help with an order, delivery, or return?

The fastest way to reach SocialEra support is by email. If you contact us about an order, include your order number, the email used at checkout, and a short description of the issue so we can respond faster.

Support At A Glance

Primary support Email support for order issues, returns, damaged items, and shipping questions.
Response target We aim to respond within 1 to 2 business days.
Best first step Send your order number and a short summary so we can route your request correctly.

Before You Contact Us

  • Include your order number if your request is about a purchase.
  • Add photos if the item arrived damaged, incorrect, or incomplete.
  • Check your tracking link first for in-transit updates.
  • Review the Shipping and Returns policies for delivery windows and eligibility rules.
For supplier-fulfilled items, extra verification may be needed before a replacement or refund can be approved.

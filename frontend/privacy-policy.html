Privacy Policy

We collect the information needed to run the store and support your orders.

This Privacy Policy explains what information SocialEra collects, how it is used, and when it may be shared with service providers or fulfillment partners in order to operate the storefront and process orders.

Privacy Summary

Information collected Order details, account information, shipping information, and site usage data.
Why it is used To process orders, support customers, improve the site, and prevent fraud or abuse.
Who may receive it Payment processors, hosting providers, analytics tools, and supplier or shipping partners when needed to fulfill orders.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, shipping address, and contact details.
  • Order history, product selections, and customer support messages.
  • Basic technical information such as browser, device, and general site activity.

How We Use Information

  • To process purchases and deliver orders.
  • To send shipping updates, support responses, and service notices.
  • To maintain account features and improve site usability.
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or policy violations.

Third-Party Services

SocialEra may use third-party services for payments, authentication, analytics, hosting, email delivery, shipping, and supplier fulfillment. Those providers may process personal information only as needed to provide their services.

Cookies And Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or similar tools to keep the site functioning, remember preferences, measure traffic, and improve performance. Disabling certain cookies may affect how the storefront works.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain personal information by contacting support, subject to legal, accounting, and operational obligations that require us to retain some records.

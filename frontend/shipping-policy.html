Shipping Policy

Delivery timing can vary by product and fulfillment source.

SocialEra offers a mix of catalog-managed and supplier-fulfilled products. Because of that, processing and delivery timelines may vary between items, and orders containing multiple products may arrive in separate packages.

Shipping Summary

Processing Most orders are processed within 2 to 5 business days unless a product page states otherwise.
Transit time Estimated shipping windows vary by destination and supplier location.
Split shipments Items in the same order may ship separately when sourced from different fulfillment partners.

Processing Times

Processing begins after your order is placed and payment is confirmed. Products fulfilled by supplier partners may require additional handling time before dispatch. Orders are not typically processed on weekends or public holidays.

Estimated Delivery Windows

  • Domestic deliveries may arrive faster than internationally sourced items.
  • Supplier-fulfilled items may take longer than inventory fulfilled directly from a local warehouse.
  • Estimated delivery dates are not guaranteed and may be affected by customs, weather, carrier disruptions, or supplier delays.

Tracking And Updates

When tracking is available, it will be sent after the order has shipped. Some suppliers take additional time to generate tracking details after a label is created.

Incorrect Address And Delivery Issues

Customers are responsible for entering a complete and accurate shipping address at checkout. If an address is entered incorrectly and the order has already been processed, rerouting or replacement may not be possible. If a package appears lost or stalled, contact us and we will review the shipment status with the carrier or supplier.

Customs, Duties, And Import Charges

For international orders, local customs duties, taxes, or import fees may apply depending on the destination country. These charges are typically the responsibility of the customer unless otherwise stated at checkout.
